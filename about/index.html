About me

I am Nicholas Trauthwein, an independent data analyst based in Kansas with a strong background in data science and related computer science topics. After studying Kinesiology at Kansas State University for two years, I pursued a career in freelance audio engineering. Later, I became aware of the field of analytics, and I became intrigued by its multidisciplinary nature. I have since devoted myself to learning everything I can about this field and have been creating projects that showcase my skills across various roles in the data community. You can find my projects hosted here on my personal portfolio website.


Although data analytics is my primary field of occupational interest, I am passionate about many other pursuits such as music production, exercise, cooking, and camping. I find these activities to be excellent outlets for creativity and stress relief. I am also interested in the domain of software development. In the future, I plan to create and publish a range of applications, some of which may not necessarily relate to data science. My goal is to expand my knowledge and skills in software development and explore new avenues of innovation.

About this site

I designed and developed this portfolio website using CSS, HTML, and JS. Some libraries and visual templates utilized in the creation of the site include Bootstrap and Hugo. The general web files are hosted on my Github profile, which is linked in the sidebar to the right. You can access the specific project files on their respective pages or on the repository. The website serves the purpose of showcasing my skills in the analytics and computer science field, as well as practicing my web development abilities.
